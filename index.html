Heat Settings for Your Rooms

This is a very basic application for one person living in a house with four separate rooms (bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, livingroom) for winter. This is only made for weekdays when he/she wakes up, goes to work, return back, have dinner, and then sleep.

Several assumptions has been made regarding the room where he/she performs certain daily tasks such as dinner in the kitchen, and not in the living room

The day starts at 0.00 and ends at 23:00. 6.00 represents time between 6:00 - 6.59.

The temperature settings are 0 for heating is OFF, 100 for heating is ON (the person preferred temperature), and 50 for bearable temperate for few minutes (going quickly to bathroom during night) The person has to sleep on or before 23:00.

How it works?

Please select your daily routine below. Then check out the savings to your heating.The saving result for each room denotes the percentage of hours in a day when the heating was turned OFF. 100 means that heating was turned OFF during the entire day.

Change sliders to adjust time.
